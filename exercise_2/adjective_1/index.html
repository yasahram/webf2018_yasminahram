PROJECT: FIRST ISSUE OF ZINE.

soap: a zine about showering.

soap is a zine that documents my friends’ showering routines through a series of photos and bits of conversation between us. showering is personally one of my favorite things to do and i’ve recently realized how specific and particular my routine is to my friends’. soap came about by wanting to highlight the discovery of our differences and similarities in showering, and embracing the unique quirks, loosely inspire by this ITG story. it’s about my friends, for my friends. soap is an embodiment of our friendships and dynamics.
